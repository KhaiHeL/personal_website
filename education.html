My Education

It took me ten minutes to walk there from my residence.


After I finished my studies in primary school, I skipped to secondary school called Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Laloh.
While studying in that school I got many memories that I can't forget, among which I did the art coursework that made batik at that time.

As long as I continue my studies at the university, I enjoy meeting good friends who help me a lot throughout my studies
